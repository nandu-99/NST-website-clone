Eligibility Criteria

Newton School of Technology is on the lookout for students who possess the capabilities to spearhead future technological
innovations. Our mission is to nurture and cultivate the upcoming generation of pioneering leaders destined to mold the future. As
such, we evaluate every applicant according to the following criteria:

Academics

Applicants must have completed Class 12th with PCM in 2022, 2023
or 2024 from a recognised board.

Online NSAT Exam

Applicants must have secured a minimum score in each section of
the Newton SAT, an online entrance exam.

Personal Interview and GD

Applicants must clear the offline Interview and Group Discussion
rounds, where our experienced admissions committee will assess
your personality and culture-fit for the tech industry.

FAQs

Everything you need to know about the program. Got
more questions? Get in touch.

How do I apply?

You can apply to the course here. In case of any other clarification required, please
contact our support team.

Does the program grant me status as Rishihood University
alumni?

Yes, you will be given alumni status of both Rishihood University and Newton School
of Technology.

Is this course approved by UGC?

Yes, this course is approved by UGC.

What is the eligibility criteria?

Applicants must have completed Class 12th with PCM in 2022, 2023 or 2024 from a
recognised board.

What is the fee structure?

The fee for the entire course inclusive of tuition fees, hostel & mess is ₹2,76,250 per
semester.

What is the admission and selection process?

You can register on our website here. After registration, you will have to pay the exam
fee of ₹1200 for NSAT - the online qualifying entrance test to the course, and then
appear for the test on the given date and slots. Those who qualify the test will be then
forwarded for the personal interview and group discussion round.

Can I have exit options during the course?

There are no exit options available. You will get a valid degree only when you complete
the 4 year courses satisfying all the degree requirements.

What qualification does one get after graduating from Newton
School of Technology?

After completing the 4-year programme, you get a UGC and AICTE recognised B.
Tech. degree in Computer Science & AI. This degree enables you to apply for
government jobs or Indian and overseas higher studies, if you wish to. Along with a
Newton School of Technology and Rishihood University certificate, recognized by
over 2000 tech companies globally, you will also be eligible to apply for any Master's
degree.

I ran into an error or issue in the application form. What should
I do?

Don't worry, you can always send a message to our support desk through the chatbot
or our WhatsApp number given here. If the error is after payment has been made,
please forward the confirmation email or payment screenshot along with email ID,
application number to support.nst@newtonschool.co.
