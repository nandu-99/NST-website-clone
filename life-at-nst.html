Life at NST

Nestled amidst the vibrant cityscape, Newton School of Technology boasts a sprawling 25-acre campus that lies at the heart of the bustling
region between Delhi and Gurgaon. Surrounded by lush greenery and serene landscapes, this cutting-edge institution stands tall, offering
students an inspiring learning environment.

With its strategic location, the campus enjoys close proximity to numerous tech parks, fostering valuable industry connections and providing
students with unparalleled opportunities to engage with the thriving tech ecosystem.

Life at Campus

The campus is a vibrant hub of cultural and intellectual activities.

The Office of Student Affairs works toward building opportunities that help students nurture their creative endeavours while striving for
excellence. The university has a sprawling 25 acre campus in Sonipat.

The campus is studded with 3 academic and administrative blocks, huge grounds, 2 hostel buildings to lodge 1000+ students and a sports arena.
The campus, with all its amenities is a mini-city in itself.

The campus follows an infrastructure that reflects our philosophy and unique brick and Jaali façade.
